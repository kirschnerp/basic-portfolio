My name is Paul Kirschner. I was the middle of 5 children, 4 boys and 1 girl. I grew up in Torrington, CT. I am married with 2 children. I love music and technology, ironically technology which ruined the music industry. I was introduced to IT when I was in the Air Force. I was attempting to get things done the old fashioned way, phone calls and visits in person. I walked into an office complaining about my lack of progress the gentleman at the desk typed on his computer and located all the parts I was looking for within several minutes. You are always told to never volunteer for anything in the service, but I volunteered to work in that office against the advice of just about everyone I know. About 3 weeks into my new job the NCO in charge came in and told me congratulations you are now the assistant LAN administrator.
